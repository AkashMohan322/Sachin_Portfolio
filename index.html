SACHIN RAMESH TENDULKAR

Sachin Tendulkar, in full Sachin Ramesh Tendulkar, (born April 24, 1973, Bombay [Mumbai], India), Indian professional cricket player, considered by many to be one of the greatest batsmen of all time. In 2012 he became the first cricketer to score 100 centuries (100 runs in a single innings) in international play.
Tendulkar was given his first bat when he was 11 years of age. As a 14-year-old, he used it to score 329 out of a world-record stand of 664 in a school match. A year later he scored a century on his first-class debut for Bombay (Mumbai), and at age 16 years 205 days he became India’s youngest Test (international) cricketer, making his debut against Pakistan in Karachi in November 1989. When he was 18 he scored two centuries in Australia (148 in Sydney and 114 in Perth), and in 1994 he scored 179 against the West Indies. In August 1996, at age 23, Tendulkar was made captain of his country’s team.
In 2012 Tendulkar became a member of the Rajya Sabha, the upper chamber of the Indian parliament—the first active athlete to join that body; he was nominated to the post, and his term ended in 2018. In 2014 he became the first sportsman to receive India’s highest civilian honour, the Bharat Ratna. Tendulkar was inducted into the International Cricket Council Hall of Fame in 2019.
In an ODI match against Bangladesh in March 2012, Tendulkar scored his record-setting 100th international century—which included both Test (51 centuries) and ODI (49 centuries) play. He retired from ODI cricket later that year, and in 2013 he ended a six-year stint with the Indian Premier League (as a member of the Mumbai Indians) and retired from Test cricket, ending his playing days with records for the most career international runs (34,357) and Test runs (15,921).

Works

Children & Health

In 2003, Tendulkar worked for UNICEF's initiative to spread awareness about polio prevention in India. In 2010, Tendulkar's campaign for the Crusade Against Cancer Foundation, "Sachin's Crusade Against Cancer in Children", raised ₹10.25 million .In November 2021, he donated retinal cameras, which can be used to diagnose retinopathy of prematurity, to a hospital in Assam.

Children & Education

On 18 September 2011, Tendulkar spent nine hours on the 12-hour "Coca-Cola-NDTV Support My School" telethon. The campaign raised ₹70 million for "the creation of basic facilities, particularly toilets for girl students, in 140 government schools across the country".

Children & Sports

Sport does not differentiate once we enter the arena. It's a level-playing field that allows everyone to flourish and prepares the individuals for various life skills. Sports as a leveller teaches us how to deal with success and failure.

Batting for charity

Thanks to the master blaster, over 200 underprivileged children are being sponsored for free education. A Mumbai-based NGO, Apnalaya, is the beneficiary. Along with education, Sachin also sponsors articles like stationery and uniforms for the students.

Writings

'Sachin: Cricketer of the Century' by Vimal Kumar

The book includes accounts from bigwigs in the cricketing field and covers each of Tendulkar's matches and his batting career, which has seen its ups and downs. It shows you that his wins outweigh his losses substantially and that's what makes him the greatest cricketer in the history of the game.

'Master Laster' by Sumit Chakraberty

There aren't many books out there that look at Sachin in an overtly critical fashion because his achievements are widely acclaimed by numerous fans. This book seeks to overcome such a social stigma and look at Sachin Tendulkar's career in an objective manner.

'Master Stroke' by Neelima Athalye

The book is dedicated to Sachin's fans who would love to bring back those memories and go back to those wonderful moments in the form of this book. Each match Sachin played was special and each century he made was even more special for his fans

'Sachin' by Gulu Ezekiel

At the young age of 17, Tendulkar earned the title of being the second-youngest man to score a hundred runs in international cricket. This book traces the life and achievements of this individual, who is hailed as being the finest batsman in the world.
